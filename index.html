About Us

DC Medical Clinic is a New Walk-In/Primary Care Medical Clinic where we offer the best care and have the best staff. We also provide a Spanish Interpreter on site. We are located just off Vann Drive on Stonebridge right next door to IHOP.

The Medical Clinic is owned by Dr. William Carney Jr, MD and operated by Deborah Leggett, MSN, FNP-C where her Passion is Medicine and being Compassionate with her patients.

Our Mission

To improve the health of the people served by providing high quality care, a comprehensive range of services, convenient and timely access with exceptional service and compassion.

At DC Medical Services we provide primary care services, as well as the convenience of Walk-In to all age groups.
